About me

I am currently a CS Master student at Georgia Tech , specializing in computer system and high performance computing. Before I moving to United States, I obtained my Honored Bachelor Degree from Shandong University and spent a year at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology as a research postgrduate student focusing on code conprehension and regeneration.

My research instests lies in System / HPC / Parallel Computing / ML System and I am very passionate about various acceleration technologies.
